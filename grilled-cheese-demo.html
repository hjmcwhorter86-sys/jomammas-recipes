Classic Grilled Cheese

The ultimate comfort sandwich — golden, crispy bread with perfectly melted cheese. Simple, satisfying, and ready in minutes.

Demo
Quick Comfort Food Vegetarian
Servings: 1 sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices sturdy sandwich bread (sourdough or white)
  • 1–2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 2–3 slices American or cheddar cheese (or a mix)

Instructions

  1. Spread softened butter evenly on one side of each bread slice and let rest for 1 minute so the butter soaks in.
  2. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium-low heat for 2 minutes until evenly warmed.
  3. Place one bread slice butter-side down in the skillet, layer on the cheese slices, then top with the second slice butter-side up. Press gently. Cook for 3 minutes without moving so a golden crust forms.
  4. Peek at the bottom — it should be deep golden. Flip carefully and cook for another 2 minutes until the second side matches.
  5. Remove from heat and let rest for 30 seconds before cutting so the cheese sets slightly and doesn't ooze everywhere.

Notes

  • Low and slow is the secret — medium-low heat gives the cheese time to melt before the bread burns.
  • American cheese melts the most smoothly; sharp cheddar adds more flavor. A blend of both is a crowd-pleaser.
  • Cover the skillet with a lid for the first side to help the cheese melt faster.
