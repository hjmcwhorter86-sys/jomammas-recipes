About JoMamma

JoMamma, Heather Jo, created this website as a way to find and cook her favorite recipes without messy ads and long stories making them difficult to follow. Each recipe is designed to be like a simple page in a cookbook or an old-fashioned recipe card.

In 2025, Heather began a personal weight-loss and wellness journey, which inspired her to rework many of her family’s favorite meals to be lighter in calories and higher in protein — without sacrificing flavor.

While not every recipe here is “diet food” (because sometimes you just need real comfort food), most include estimated calories and protein so you can make choices that fit your goals and your family.

And hey — if you want to swap skim milk for whole or light spread for real butter, nobody’s judging. These recipes are meant to be flexible, forgiving, and enjoyed your way. Live your best life.
